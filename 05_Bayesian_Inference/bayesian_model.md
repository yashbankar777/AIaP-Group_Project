# Bayesian Model Notes

This module should define how evidence is combined.

Possible inputs:

- extraction confidence from Member 1
- KG status from Member 4
- KG confidence from Member 4
- optional LIAR speaker history counts

Possible output labels:

- `likely true`
- `likely false`
- `uncertain`

The report should explain the assumptions behind the probability calculation and why uncertainty is retained rather than forcing every claim into a binary answer.
